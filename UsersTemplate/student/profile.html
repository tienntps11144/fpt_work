gps_fixed Mục tiêu nghề nghiệp

a) Sales support, customer service, delivery coordinator task:


- Work as a window person, keep the smooth information flow between Vietnam customers, foreign factories, forwader, head quarter as well as foreign branchs in the Group.

- Use English to communicate and work with all members and foreign partners in Singapore, Malaysia, Japan, Korea, Taiwan, Hongkong, Thailand.. via email, phone call, and skype chat…

- Go business trip (overseas and domestic trip) when assigned.

- Build and maintain the good relationship with customers, take care and try best to get customer's maximum satisfaction.

- Visit customer to have face to face meeting when neccessary.

- Coordinate closely with related members to find the best solution for any arising issue like NG goods, shortage quantity...

 - Negotiate, persuade customer and factory for any changing or urgent customer request like increase or decrease quantity, pull in or postpone shipment, combine or partial shipment with the short lead time as well as other customer special request..

- Support to prepare sales contract, quotation...

- Follow customer new orders, sales contracts and forcasts, check to make sure the accuracy of price, delivery term ...before making order to factory.

 - Manage delivery schedules tightly (EXW, ETD, ETA), get vessel booking confirmation, advise customer and communicate relevant information.

 - Handle the processing of all orders and shipments with accuracy and timeliness.

 - Inform clients of unforeseen delays or problems.

 - Collect and check the set of shipping docs for Sea, Air, and Truck shipment (Invoice, packing list, C/O, C/Q, COC, BL, AWB, Proof of delivery...), provide customer for doing customs clearance.


b) Admin task:


- Assist to obtain Visa, work permit, temporary resident card for expatriates as well as hotel, flight booking.

- Recieve and answer comming call to office.

- Send and receive parcel.

-Manage file, stationary and other admin tasks when assigned.


c) Account task:


 - Assist for account tasks (collect, check the accuracy and proper of red invoice and the other account supporting docs.

- Check bank statement, staff’s claims...

- Prepare payment voucher to make payment to supplier on time.
